Intermediate: 500-750mg per week

Effects & Benefits

Increased Muscle Mass

Enhanced protein synthesis leading to increased muscle growth and strength gains.

Improved Recovery

Faster recovery between training sessions and reduced muscle soreness.

Enhanced Performance

Increased strength, power output, and athletic performance.

Side Effects

Important Safety Information

Regular blood work monitoring is essential. Watch for these potential side effects and consult healthcare professionals when needed.

Estrogen-Related

Gynecomastia, water retention, increased blood pressure.

Androgenic

Acne, male pattern baldness, body hair growth.

HPTA Suppression

Decreased natural testosterone production, requiring proper PCT.

PCT Requirements

Standard PCT protocol following testosterone cycle:

  • Wait 2 weeks after last testosterone injection (if long ester)
  • Nolvadex: 40/40/20/20 mg per day (weeks 1-4)
  • Clomid: 100/100/50/50 mg per day (weeks 1-4)
  • HCG: 1000IU EOD for 2 weeks before starting SERMs
