First Cycle Guide

This guide is designed for individuals considering their first anabolic steroid cycle. It emphasizes safety, proper protocol, and responsible use.

Prerequisites

  • Minimum age of 25 years
  • At least 3-4 years of consistent training experience
  • Proper diet and nutrition fundamentals established
  • Recent bloodwork completed
  • Understanding of PCT (Post Cycle Therapy) protocols
  • All necessary medications and supplements on hand before starting

Recommended First Cycle

Testosterone Enanthate or Cypionate Only

  • Weeks 1-12: 500mg Testosterone per week (split into 2 injections)
  • AI (Aromatase Inhibitor): Only if needed, based on symptoms
  • HCG (optional): 250IU twice per week throughout cycle

Required Items

Primary Compounds

  • Testosterone Enanthate or Cypionate
  • Anastrozole (AI) - have on hand
  • HCG (optional but recommended)

PCT Medications

  • Nolvadex (Tamoxifen)
  • Clomid (Clomiphene) - optional

Cycle Protocol

During Cycle (Weeks 1-12)

  • Inject 250mg testosterone every 3.5 days
  • Monitor for side effects
  • Conduct mid-cycle bloodwork (Week 6-7)
  • Maintain proper diet and training program
  • Use AI only if experiencing estrogen-related side effects

Post Cycle Therapy (Weeks 13-18)

  • Wait 2 weeks after last testosterone injection
  • Weeks 1-6 of PCT: Nolvadex 20mg daily
  • Optional: Add Clomid 25mg daily
  • Maintain caloric intake and training intensity
  • Get bloodwork 6-8 weeks after PCT

Side Effects Management

Common Side Effects

  • Acne
  • Mood changes
  • Water retention
  • Increased blood pressure
  • Gynecomastia (if estrogen not managed)

Health Monitoring

Regular health monitoring is crucial. Get bloodwork:

  • Before starting the cycle
  • Mid-cycle (Week 6-7)
  • 6-8 weeks after PCT

Key markers to monitor:

  • Testosterone (Total and Free)
  • Estradiol
  • Liver enzymes (AST/ALT)
  • Lipids
  • Hematocrit
  • Blood pressure (regularly throughout cycle)

Important Warnings

  • Never start a cycle without having PCT medications on hand
  • Always obtain medications from reliable sources
  • Keep all medications safely stored and away from others
  • Maintain regular contact with healthcare providers
  • Stop cycle if experiencing severe side effects
