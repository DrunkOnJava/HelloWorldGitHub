Post Cycle Therapy (PCT) Protocols

Post Cycle Therapy (PCT) is crucial for maintaining gains and restoring natural hormone production after an anabolic steroid cycle. This guide provides comprehensive protocols for effective recovery.

Understanding PCT

PCT is designed to help your body restore its natural testosterone production after a cycle of anabolic steroids. Without proper PCT, you risk losing gains and experiencing prolonged hormonal imbalance.

When is PCT Needed?

PCT is required after:

  • Any cycle containing testosterone
  • Most injectable anabolic steroids
  • Oral steroid cycles

PCT may not be needed for:

  • TRT (Testosterone Replacement Therapy) if continuing
  • Some specific compounds (consult healthcare provider)

Standard PCT Protocols

Basic Protocol (8 weeks)

  • Weeks 1-2: Nolvadex 40mg daily
  • Weeks 3-4: Nolvadex 30mg daily
  • Weeks 5-6: Nolvadex 20mg daily
  • Weeks 7-8: Nolvadex 10mg daily

Advanced Protocol (with Clomid)

  • Weeks 1-2: Nolvadex 40mg + Clomid 50mg daily
  • Weeks 3-4: Nolvadex 30mg + Clomid 25mg daily
  • Weeks 5-6: Nolvadex 20mg daily
  • Weeks 7-8: Nolvadex 10mg daily

Timing Your PCT

When to Start PCT

  • Short-ester testosterone: 3 days after last injection
  • Long-ester testosterone: 2 weeks after last injection
  • Nandrolone or Trenbolone: 2-3 weeks after last injection
  • Oral steroids: 24 hours after last dose

PCT Medications

Primary Medications

  • Nolvadex (Tamoxifen)
  • Clomid (Clomiphene)
  • HCG (pre-PCT)

Supporting Supplements

  • Vitamin D3
  • Zinc
  • Magnesium
  • Fish Oil

Recovery Optimization

During PCT:

  • Maintain caloric intake at maintenance or slight surplus
  • Continue regular training with moderate intensity
  • Ensure adequate sleep (8+ hours)
  • Manage stress levels
  • Monitor for signs of low testosterone

Monitoring Recovery

Get bloodwork to check:

  • Testosterone levels (Total and Free)
  • Estradiol
  • LH and FSH
  • Complete metabolic panel
  • Lipid profile

Recommended testing schedule:

  • Pre-cycle baseline
  • Mid-cycle (if running longer cycles)
  • 6-8 weeks after PCT completion

Important Warnings

  • Never start a cycle without having PCT medications on hand
  • Avoid alcohol during PCT
  • Watch for signs of depression or mood changes
  • Seek medical attention if experiencing severe side effects
  • Consider time between cycles (Time on + PCT = Time off)
